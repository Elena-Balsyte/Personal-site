A little bit about me

Hello World! I am a self-starter Front-end developer. My journey into tech started when I moved to London as a single mum. I needed to find a way to work and take care of my child. So I decided that the best option for me was to work remotely. Being a person that loves learning new things, I knew that I would enjoy a job that allows me to work on projects that require the ability to learn fast and stay focused for a long time. So I dived into the digital world and I spent months browsing and reading about all the opportunities that the World Wide Web can bring for you.

I took some online courses and learned about the fundamentals of digital marketing, I practiced using Adobe Photoshop and created a few websites with WordPress and Elementor builder. Then it was time for HTML and CSS. I learned how to write semantic code and how to use the version control system Git. My journey of becoming a developer continues and I am excited and motivated to study and practice every day. I am open to opportunities in the tech world and I am motivated to become a part of a team that develops this amazing Digital World.

Hand-coded HTML and well organized CSS

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible. I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Ease Converting Designs into Code

You can trust me to take a designer's PSD and quickly & accurately convert it into a webpage that is a pixel-perfect match. One of my goals this year is to improve my design skills and learn all that takes to create a beautiful piece of art which can be coded.

Creating websites with WordPress.

Need a website that is a quick solution and ready to edit whenever you need it? Choosing WordPress allows using hundreds of ready-made templates and plugins to create a website with beautiful design and great functionality.
